App Features

business

You Decide

Set up your business and all of your locations inside the app! Every business is unique and we let you decide how you manage your inventory. Do-it-yourself or delegate to your team.

manage_accounts

Control Access

Add as many Managers and staffmembers as you like to access each location. Then, decide who does what with clearly defined roles among all of your staff.

sell

Customize Your Products

Inventory app keeps track of 10 different data points on every product in your inventory. Keep track of your products and never get blindsided again.

dashboard

Stay Up to Date

In an upcoming release, Inventory dashboard will give you everything you need at a glance. Check on the latest inventory values across all locations, see alerts and check the status of ongoing inventory counts.

insights

Plan Ahead

In another upcoming release, Inventory orders will give you all your orders by vendor based on your set pars and current inventory levels.
